About Ian Gorrie

Three decades of security. One simple mission: Enable business while managing risk intelligently.

  • 1990s

    The Early Days

    Started as a software developer in the early days of the commercial internet. Built systems, wrote code, and learned how applications work in production—not just in theory. This technical foundation became invaluable when moving into security.

  • 1998

    Locked Networks Founded

    Founded Locked Networks to help organizations navigate emerging security challenges. The name came from a simple idea: security should be like a well-designed lock—reliable, understandable, and effective without being unnecessarily complex.

  • 2000s-2010s

    Professional Development & Growth

    Pursued rigorous professional development through (ISC)², ISACA, and ISSA. Became OWASP Chapter Leader in Seattle. Spoke at the Federal Trade Commission on application security—gaining deep insight into how regulators think about security and compliance.

  • 2020s

    The AI Era

    Now helping organizations navigate AI security, privacy compliance, and modern threat landscapes. Currently active at Puzzle (since 2022), dealing with modern threats every day. Not living off past experience—continuously learning and adapting.
